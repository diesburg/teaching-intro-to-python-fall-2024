Design Document for program admit.py

General Description: This program determines if a student can attend Whatsamatta U. based on ACT and GPA scores.

Inputs: ACT exam score and high school GPA

Calculation: Check that the student has at least 23 on the ACT and a GPA of at least 1.75.  If either score is too low, we print student cannot be admitted.

Outputs: Prints message showing if student can be admitted
